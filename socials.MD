# Social Media Strategy — Thryft

## 1. Product Name & Category
**Product:** Thryft
**Category:** E-commerce / Marketplace for Premium Pre-owned Electronics

## 2. Target Audience
**Who are we selling to?**
- Tech-savvy individuals in India (students, young professionals).
- Bargain hunters looking for premium Apple products (iPhones, MacBooks, Watches).
- Eco-conscious consumers interested in the circular economy.

## 3. Primary Distribution Channels
- **Instagram:** Highly visual platform for showcasing product condition and unboxing.
- **WhatsApp:** Direct sales, trust-building, and customer support.
- **OLX/Quikr:** Leveraging existing marketplaces where target users search for used electronics.
- **YouTube:** In-depth reviews, "Condition Grade" explanations, and unboxing videos.

## 4. Content Strategy
- **Unboxing Pre-owned Gems:** High-quality videos showing exactly what a "Grade A" device looks like.
- **Price Comparison:** "New vs. Thryft" price breakdowns to highlight savings.
- **Condition Checks:** Educational content on how we verify and grade our devices.
- **Customer Video Testimonials:** Real users sharing their experience and their "new" device.

## 5. Growth Hack / Viral Loop
**The "Upgrade & Earn" Loop:**
Offer an instant valuation and "Buyback" program for users' old devices when they purchase from Thryft. Combine this with a "Refer a Friend" program where both parties get ₹500 off their next purchase, incentivizing the community to bring in both buyers and sellers.
